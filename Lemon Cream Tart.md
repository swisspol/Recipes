Lemon Cream Tart
================

Ingredient | Quantity
---|---
[Sweet tart shell](Sweet Tart Shell.md) (cooled) | 1
[Lemon cream](Lemon Cream.md) | 2 1/4 cups
Heavy cream (very cold) | 1 cup
Sugar | 1 tsp

1. Pour lemon cream into tart shell
2. Put in refrigerator until firm (1-2 hours)
3. Whip cream with whisk in mixing bowl until thickened then add sugar and whip until cream holds soft peaks
4. Top tart with whipped cream

*Serve tart cool.*
